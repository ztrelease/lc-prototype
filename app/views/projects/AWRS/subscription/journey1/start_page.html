Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS) application

The Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS) applies to existing, and new, wholesalers of alcohol, trading at or after the point at which excise duty has become payable. All businesses who sell alcohol will need to make sure that any of their UK wholesalers are registered with HMRC.

Use this service if you are:

  • an alcohol wholesaler
  • a broker
  • an auctioneer
  • an alcohol retailer

Before you start

Make sure you have read the information relating to AWRS before completing the application. You will need to sign in with your Government Gateway account. If you don't have a Government Gateway account you can sign up on the next page.

Apply for Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS)

The Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS) applies to existing, and new, wholesalers of alcohol, trading at or after the point at which excise duty has become payable. All businesses who sell alcohol will need to make sure that any of their UK wholesalers are registered with HMRC. Make sure you have read the information relating to AWRS before completing the application.

Use this service if you are:

  • an alcohol wholesaler
  • a broker
  • an auctioneer

Before you start

To complete the application you will need:

  • your company UTR number
  • the addresses of any additional premises
  • the full names, national insurance numbers or passport numbers of all business directors connected to alcohol wholesale
  • the names and addresses of up to five of your main alcohol suppliers

You will need to sign in with your Government Gateway account. If you don't have a Government Gateway account you can sign up on the next page.

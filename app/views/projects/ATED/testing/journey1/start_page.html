Claiming relief on high value residential properties owned by companies

Who is elegible?

Use this service if all the following apply; your property is:

  • residential (a single dwelling interest) situated in the UK
  • valued at more than Â£1 million from 1 April 2015
  • owned completely or partly, by a company, partnership where one of the partners is a company, or 'collective investment vehicle' - for example, a unit trust or an open ended investment company

What do you need to do?

Claim relief involve the following steps:

  • register your business for an ATED account
  • designate an agent to add relief properties on belhalf of you (optional)
  • add relief properties

Before you start

Make sure you have read ATED returns notice before completing a return.

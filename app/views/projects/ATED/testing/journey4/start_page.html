Claim reliefs on high value residential properties owned by companies

Who is eligible?

Use this service if the following apply to your property. It is:

  • residential and based in the UK
  • was valued at more than Â£1 million on 1 April 2012 or at acquisition if later for returns from 2015 to 2016 onwards
  • owned completely or partly, by a company, partnership where one of the partners is a company, or 'collective investment vehicle' - for example, a unit trust or an open ended investment company

What do you need to do?

To claim, you must:

  • register your business for ATED
  • select the relief types for your properties

To appoint an agent to act on your behalf:

  • firstly, your agent must login and register their business for ATED
  • they will receive an agent reference number that they must give to you
  • you must then register your business for ATED
  • enter the agent reference number when prompted
  • you must then notify your agent of the request
  • your agent will then accept your request and act on your behalf

Before you start

Make sure you have read ATED guidance before completing a return.
